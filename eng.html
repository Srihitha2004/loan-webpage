About Us

We are a dedicated team of five Computer Science Engineering students committed to simplifying the loan process. Our project, "Loan Genie," was born out of a shared vision to eliminate the complexities and inefficiencies of traditional loan applications. We believe in empowering individuals by providing a streamlined, user-friendly platform that connects users directly with banks, removing the need for middlemen.
